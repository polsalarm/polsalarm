Philippines, completing a BS in Computer Science (Software Engineering) at FEU
Tech with an expected graduation in 2027. He is a CCSMA Dean's Lister, earning
Gold in July 2025 and Bronze in April 2025.

he writes Rust (Soroban) smart contracts: PalengkePay brings gasless
micropayments and on-chain buy-now-pay-later credit to informal Philippine
market vendors, and VeracityLink issues tamper-proof academic credentials via

NegosyoNav is a civic-tech PWA whose Gemini-powered co-pilot auto-fills
government AcroForms for micro-entrepreneurs, and Ka Asenso is a voice-commerce
marketplace driven by an AI sales agent. ScoutGrid combines eSports scouting
with DeFi primitives — trustless buyouts, protocol royalties, and collateralized
lending — on Soroban.

ISO/IEC 25010 quality score from IT experts and developers.
